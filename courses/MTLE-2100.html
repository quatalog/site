Struc Of Engr Materials

MTLE-2100

The first course in Materials Science and Engineering. Structures of metals, ceramics, and polymers and experimental techniques for their determination are discussed. Laboratory experience is included.

4 credits
Prereqs:
one of:
ENGR-1600 Materials Science For Engineer PHYS-1600 Materials Science CHEM-1600 Materials Science ISCI-1600 Materials Science BMED-2100 Biomaterials Sci & Engineering
