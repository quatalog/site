Markets, Strategy, and People

BUSN-6103

In this three-credit, 15-week online graduate course, perform strategic analyses of an organization's market position relative to its competitors and customer needs. Analyze the present state of the market relative to its strategic vision, given the organization's competitive advantage. Articulate goals and tactics that move the organization from today to tomorrow's objectives. Develop highly effective teams to implement strategies and new products.

3 credits
