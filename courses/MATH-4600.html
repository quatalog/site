Advanced Calculus

MATH-4600

A course emphasizing advanced concepts and methods from calculus. Topics include: multivariable integral theorems (Green's, divergence, Stokes', Reynolds transport), extrema of multivariable functions (including Taylor's theorem and Lagrange multipliers), the calculus of variations (EulerâLagrange equations, constraints, principle of least action), and Cartesian tensors (calculus, invariants, representations).

4 credits
Prereqs:
one of:
