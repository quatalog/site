Architectural Desgin Studio 7

ARCH-4790

This fifth-year architectural design studio employs a design research format to investigate a range of contemporary topics of interest to the discipline, including advanced fabrication, institutional & civic buildings, urban design, landscape architecture, and community stakeholders. Students apply for this studio based on their personal interest and professional objectives.. This course is offered in the fall semester and is required of all architecture undergraduates in the B.Arch. program.

5 credits
Prereqs:
ARCH-4780 Architectural Design Studio 6
