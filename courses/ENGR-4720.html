Manufacturing Processes and Systems Laboratory II

ENGR-4720

Student teams finalize manufacturing system plans from select ENGR 4710 Â projects, build a manufacturing system to complete several hundred units of a product, optimize the system, manage a project budget, modify manufacturing and packaging plans, and conform to a project schedule as proposed in the associated project Technical Data Packages produced by ENGR 4710 Â teams. In addition, students will learn new advanced manufacturing topics to increase their breath and depth of manufacturing.

4 credits Writing Intensive
